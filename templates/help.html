Help & Ethics
Frequently Asked Questions
What does each triage colour mean?
RED — Immediate: life-threatening, see doctor now.
YELLOW — Urgent: serious but stable, assess within 30 min.
GREEN — Non-urgent: minor, can wait in standard queue.
How is the triage prediction made?
Priority Care uses a machine-learning model trained on clinical triage data. When the trained model is unavailable, a rule-based fallback using clinically validated thresholds (pain ≥ 8, SpO2 ≤ 94%, resp rate ≥ 25) is applied automatically.
Is the AI prediction a medical diagnosis?
No. The triage prediction is a decision-support tool only. All clinical decisions must be made by qualified medical professionals. The system is intended to assist, not replace, clinical judgement.
How do I add a new patient?
Click + New Patient from the Dashboard or Patients page. Fill in the vitals, click Predict Triage to get the AI result, then click Save Patient to record the visit.
How do I export patient data?
Go to the Analytics page and click Export CSV. All patient records will download as a CSV file.
Ethics & Data Privacy

Priority Care is developed as an academic project by TriageAI Developers at SAIT. Patient data entered in this system is stored locally for demonstration purposes only.


Ethical principles applied:

  • Transparency — the system explains its triage reasoning via top factors
  • Human oversight — every prediction requires staff review before acting
  • Non-maleficence — the system defaults to conservative (higher) triage when uncertain
  • Data minimisation — only clinically necessary fields are collected
